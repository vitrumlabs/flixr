Tell Flixr your mood — whether you want something cosy, thrilling, hilarious, or thought-provoking — and it surfaces movies that actually match the moment. No endless scrolling through catalogues. No decision fatigue. Just swipe, discover, and watch.

MOOD-BASED DISCOVERY
Describe how you're feeling and let Flixr do the work. Our mood search understands natural language so you can say "something fun for a rainy evening" or "a mind-bending thriller" and get results that fit.
